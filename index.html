My Local Guide to My Neighborhood

Mattheus's Recs

Chinatown Factory

Chinatown Factory is a fairly popular ice cream spot in Chinatown. So be prepared for a decent line as a good amount of tourists come here. But the ice cream here is really good and I always get my ice cream in a waffle cone. You honestly can't go wrong with any of the flavors so enjoy!


Broadway in Soho

Broadway is where you can find most stores you can think of. Now personally me, I don't shop here too often because of the amount of tourists that crowd the area. But if you are in the area, window shopping is most people's plan.


Fiat Cafe

If you're looking for a good brunch to get in between shopping, I would recommend this place. Especially the panini sandwiches which are my favorite. And if the waiter likes you, they'll give you a small complimentary chocalate cake. This is a nice and cozy spot to visit.
